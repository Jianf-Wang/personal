Biography

Jianfeng Wang is a PhD candidate in computer science department at University of Oxford, supervised by Thomas Lukasiewicz . His research interests lie at the intersection of Bayesian Deep Learning, Uncertainty Quantification, Gaussian Processes and Computer Vision. He received his B.Sc and M.Eng degrees both from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, China, in 2013 and 2016, respectively.
