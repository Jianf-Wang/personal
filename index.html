Biography

Jianfeng Wang is a PhD candidate in the computer science department at University of Oxford, supervised by Thomas Lukasiewicz . His research interests are in machine learning and computer vision. He received his B.Sc and M.Eng degrees both from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, China, in 2013 and 2016, respectively.
